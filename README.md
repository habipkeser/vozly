# Vozly

Your voice. Your life. Organized.

Vozly is a voice-first productivity app for iPhone. Press and hold the mic, speak naturally, and Vozly captures your thought — no typing required.

**Four spaces, one button:**

🎯 **Todo** — Voice-powered task management with deadlines and priorities

✏️ **Notes** — Speak your ideas, replay the original audio anytime

📖 **Diary** — Daily voice journaling without the blank page

🌙 **Dreams** — Capture dreams in seconds before they fade
